⛺️ AT Trail Planning Guide

62 shelters • 11 resupply towns • Springer Mountain to Damascus, VA • 469 miles

⚠️ Great Smoky Mountains: NO RESUPPLY for ~62 miles From Fontana Dam (mile 164.7) to Davenport Gap (mile 227.3) there are no towns, no roads out, and no stores. Most hikers need 5–6 days of food. This is the longest resupply gap on the entire AT south of New England.

⚙️ Configure Your Hike

Typical NOBO: 10–15 mi/day in GA/NC terrain. Blood Mtn and Smokies ridgeline are slower.
Standard pack holds 5–7 days (4–6 lbs/day). Smokies require at least 6 days.
Max carry range: 60 miles — segments beyond this are flagged.
