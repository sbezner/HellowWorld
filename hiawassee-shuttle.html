Getting to Hiawassee, GA

From the Appalachian Trail — Shuttles, Hostels & Resupply

On trail right now? Call Stanimal's (407-435-0294) or text Encourager Shuttles (806-897-7281) — both cover Dick's Creek Gap and Unicoi Gap.

Nearest Trail Access Points

Dick's Creek Gap

AT Mile 69.4 NOBO — US Hwy 76

11 miles from Hiawassee by road. Busiest highway crossing in Georgia — best spot to hitch. Most shuttles check here morning and midday.

Unicoi Gap

AT Mile 52.5 NOBO — GA Hwy 75

10 miles from Hiawassee by road. Quieter road than US-76 — harder to hitch but several hostels offer direct pickup here.

Shuttle Options

City of Hiawassee Free Shuttle
FREE Seasonal
The City of Hiawassee runs a free daily shuttle during peak NOBO season (roughly late February through early May). Picks up at both Dick's Creek Gap and Unicoi Gap and drops off in town.
ATC Info Page
Stanimal's Around the Bend (Hostel Shuttle)
FREE for guests
Formerly Top of Georgia Hostel. Offers free pickup and drop-off at both Unicoi Gap and Dick's Creek Gap for hostel guests. Non-guests can arrange paid shuttles. Runs Amicalola Falls to Fontana Dam. Pet friendly.
Call 407-435-0294
Bandits AT Shuttles (Mountain Trails Shuttles)
PAID
Run by Dale "Bandit" Johnson, 2022 AT thru-hiker. Covers all AT access points from Amicalola Falls through the Great Smoky Mountains. Upfront pricing. Pet friendly. Text often works better than calling — he may be in poor cell areas running shuttles.
Call 828-231-7728 Text Website

Based in Hiawassee. Donation-only for local shuttles; reasonable donation for longer trips. Covers Springer to Fontana. All donations go back to the hiker community via trail magic and rescues. Text preferred.

Hitchhiking

Dick's Creek Gap (US-76) is the best hitch in Georgia — busy highway, good shoulder, and drivers are accustomed to picking up hikers. Unicoi Gap (GA-75) is quieter and harder. Thumb on the right side of the road heading west toward Hiawassee. Average wait at Dick's Creek is 15–30 minutes in season.
