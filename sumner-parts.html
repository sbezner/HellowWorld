Load Line (Cable) Assembly

783628 • 783629 • 783630 • 784790
Most wear-prone component on any Sumner lift. High repeat-purchase rate — every lift needs a new cable eventually. Lead with model number to match the right SKU.
The wire rope cable running through the mast to the lifting carriage. Daily friction against pulleys, jobsite grit, and occasional overloading all fray the cable. Sumner's own operator manual flags cable inspection as a mandatory pre-use check.

Pulley / Sheave Assembly

783921 (supersedes obsolete 783812)
Often sold alongside a new cable — a worn pulley destroys a new cable fast. Upsell the Pulley Kit from Energized Engines (includes guard + shims) for ~$106.
Sits at the top of the mast and redirects the load line. The internal bearing wears out from repetitive loading and the aluminum wheel develops groove wear that damages the cable. Part #783812 is now obsolete — replacement is #783921.

Winch Handle Replacement Kit

784841 • 784842 • 783761 • 780244
Primary operator interface — when this fails the lift is down. Customers need it fast. Two-handle vs. one-handle depends on the series; always confirm. Good margin item.
Handle, shaft, and brake components are a matched assembly — sold as a kit. Handles bend, break, or lose ratchet/brake function under daily use. Sumner's manual specifically calls out inspecting ratchet dogs and brake ratchet for wear.

Outrigger / Stabilizer Leg Assembly

783935 • 783936 • 783940 • 783945
Highest ticket item on the list — good margin. Safety-critical: without outriggers the lift can't be used at full height. Also pitch the lower-cost lock kit (783945) as a quick add-on.
Fold-out stabilizer legs that bolt to the base to prevent tipping at full height. Take repeated impact on jobsites — locking pins and leg tubes are most commonly damaged. #783935 for the 2000/2100 series is the most widely stocked and frequently ordered.
Part Numbers:

  • 783940 — Outrigger Lock Assembly (2000/2100 & 2400) — lower-cost subcomponent
  • 783945 — Outrigger Lock Kit (2000/2100) — good add-on upsell
$418 – $700
