Rock Gap Shelter

North Carolina  ·  AT Mile ~101.5  ·  Lean-to  ·  Elev. ~3,750 ft

Franklin Town Access Point Rock Gap (Winding Stair Gap) is the primary road crossing for the Franklin, NC resupply. US-64 at this gap provides reliable hitchhiking to Franklin ~10 miles west — average wait 15–30 min in season. This is the most important road crossing between Georgia and the NOC.

Rock Gap Shelter sits just 0.25 miles north of Winding Stair Gap on US-64 — the de facto resupply gateway for Franklin, NC. This makes Rock Gap one of the most strategically important shelters in the southern AT. Many thru-hikers stay here the night before a Franklin resupply, or use it as a stopping point right after returning from town. The lean-to sleeps 8 with tent pads nearby and is maintained by the Nantahala Hiking Club.

At 3,750 feet — lower than the shelters to the south — Rock Gap benefits from milder temperatures and a forest of mixed oak, pine, and tulip poplar typical of mid-elevation southern Appalachian ridges. A reliable stream runs near the shelter, providing consistent water except in the driest summers. The proximity to the highway means this shelter can be noisier than more remote options, but road access also means you can bail to town quickly if weather or injury requires it.

The shelter sees heavy traffic during peak NOBO season (March–May) as hikers funnel through for the Franklin resupply. Plan to arrive early if you want the lean-to; tent pads offer a quieter alternative. The area around Winding Stair Gap has good cell signal — a rarity in this section of the AT — so it's a good place to catch up on communications before or after town.

Cell Signal: Winding Stair Gap (US-64, 0.25 mi south) typically has usable cell signal. If you need to call the Budget Inn for a shuttle, place that call from the gap before hiking up to the shelter.

Getting Supplies — Franklin, NC

Franklin is a full trail town with a grocery store, gear shop, restaurants, laundry, and multiple hiker-friendly lodging options. This is the primary resupply between Georgia and the NOC (34+ miles north).

RECOMMENDED

Hitchhike from Winding Stair Gap (US-64)

0.25 miles south on AT to gap, then ~10 road miles west to Franklin. Walk south on the AT to the US-64 road crossing. Stick your thumb out on the west shoulder — traffic heading west toward Franklin is regular and hikers are a familiar sight here. Average wait: 15–30 minutes. Budget Inn offers a free shuttle if you call ahead. In Franklin: Ingles Supermarket (full grocery), Outdoor 76 outfitter (74 E Main St, 828-524-8801, holds mail drops free), restaurants, laundry.

ALTERNATE

Outdoor 76 Shuttle

Call from the gap: (828) 524-8801. Outdoor 76 occasionally offers or can arrange shuttle service from Winding Stair Gap. Policies change by season — call from the gap to confirm. The store is one of the best-stocked trail outfitters in the southern AT, carrying fuel canisters, food, gear, and clothing. Staff are experienced with thru-hiker needs.

Mail Drop: Outdoor 76, 74 E Main St, Franklin, NC 28734. No hold fee for thru-hikers. If mailing ahead, ship to arrive 2–3 days before you plan to pick up.

~0.25 mi south to gap + ~10 road miles west · Year-round
The hiker staple in Franklin. Free shuttle from Winding Stair Gap (call ahead), on-site laundry, hiker-familiar staff. Walking distance to Ingles Supermarket. Perfect zero-day or nero base. The Budget Inn has been welcoming AT hikers for decades and the staff know exactly what thru-hikers need — early check-in, gear storage, and no judgment about how you smell.

Franklin's hiker hostel. Bunks, shared kitchen, gear drying, communal vibe. Budget option with shuttle availability. Great for meeting other hikers. Call to reserve — this fills up fast in March and April when NOBO season peaks.
