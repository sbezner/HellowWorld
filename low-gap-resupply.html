Resupply from Low Gap Shelter

Appalachian Trail, Georgia — AT Mile ~43 from Springer • Elevation 3,050 ft

Closest exit: Hike ~9.5 miles north to Unicoi Gap (GA-75) From Unicoi Gap, Helen is 9 miles east and Hiawassee is 10 miles west. Call Stanimal's (407-435-0294) or text Encourager Shuttles (806-897-7281) for a pickup.

Your Location

Low Gap Shelter sits at AT Mile ~43 from Springer Mountain, Georgia, between Neel Gap (Mile 31.7) and Unicoi Gap (Mile 52.5). Elevation 3,050 ft. Water is located approximately 30–40 meters in front of the shelter. Privy is up the hill.

You are ~9.5 miles south of Unicoi Gap and ~11 miles north of Neel Gap (Mountain Crossings). Both exits require a solid half-day of hiking — Unicoi Gap is slightly closer.

Exit Options

RECOMMENDED

North ~9.5 mi → Unicoi Gap (GA-75)

Hike north over Blue Mountain (summit ~mile 48) and Blue Mountain Shelter (~mile 50.7) to Unicoi Gap. Strenuous climb over Blue Mountain then a descent to the gap. GA-75 has decent traffic — hitchhiking east to Helen (~9 mi) or west to Hiawassee (~10 mi) is common. Most shuttle services cover this gap.

ALTERNATE

South ~11 mi → Neel Gap (US-19/129)

Hike back south to Neel Gap. Mountain Crossings outfitter is on the trail — no shuttle needed. Good for gear and basic resupply but limited food selection compared to Hiawassee or Helen.

Where to Resupply

Hiawassee, GA (Recommended)
~10 miles west of Unicoi Gap on GA-75
The top thru-hiker resupply destination in North Georgia. Walkable Main Street with everything a hiker needs. Free city shuttle runs during peak season from Unicoi Gap.

Helen, GA
~9 miles east of Unicoi Gap on GA-75
Georgia's alpine village — popular tourist town. Good food and lodging options. Grocery store and post office available. Unicoi State Park (2 miles NE of Helen) offers camping, cabins, showers, laundry, and a restaurant.
Grocery Store Post Office Restaurants Hotels / Motels Unicoi State Park (showers + laundry)
Mountain Crossings at Neel Gap (On-Trail Option)
10 miles south on the AT — no road exit needed
If you'd rather not leave the trail, hike 10 miles south back to Neel Gap. Mountain Crossings is the only outfitter in the country where the AT passes directly through the building. Good for gear repairs, fuel, and basic food — but limited full resupply compared to Hiawassee or Helen.
